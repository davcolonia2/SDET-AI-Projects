About Me

I'm a passionate Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET) with over 7 years of experience in the quality assurance field. My expertise spans across testing mobile applications (Android, iOS) and web applications, with a strong foundation in automation testing.

Throughout my career, I've conducted 200+ functional, UI, smoke, and regression tests, ensuring high-quality product releases. I specialize in developing and executing test automation scripts using Selenium WebDriver with Java/Python, and I'm known for my exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts from UC Berkeley and completed the Berkeley Extension coding bootcamp focusing on Data Analytics using Python and API integration. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and contribute to creating robust, reliable software solutions.
